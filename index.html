Frames, India's Largest
Undergraduate Film Festival.

Frames is 3-day fest held in January every year. It's non-profit with more than
4000+ films submitted every year. We provide amateur and professional filmmakers,
from all over the world, a platform to showcase and celebrate their talents.

Highlights and Events

Live Screening Screening of the best film submissions in different categories: Short films ,Music videos, Animations, Documentaries & AD films.
Judges The screenings are judged and graded by professionals including Directors, Actors and Writers from the Film industry.
Laurel & Awards Every filmmaker whose selection is screened will receive a Laurel. The winners receive certificates and trophies in the Awards Ceremony.
Mehfilm (Pre-event) A movie night under the stars. Gentle breezes, good conversation and a magical night under the blanket of skies
CSR Initiative (World Disability Day) Team Frames visitied ETC Centre of Disability and performed various fun activites with the children there.
