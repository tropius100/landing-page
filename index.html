Frames, India's Largest
Undergraduate Film Festival.

Frames is a 3-day festival held in January of every year. It's a non-profit with
more than 4000+ films submitted every year. We provide amateur and professional filmmakers, from all over the world, a platform to showcase
and celebrate their talents.

x

Highlights and Events

Live Screening
Screening of the best film submissions in different categories: Short films ,Music videos, Animations, Documentaries & AD films.
Laurels & Awards
Every filmmaker whose selection is screened will receive a Laurel. The winners receive certificates and trophies in the Awards Ceremony.
Judges
The screenings are judged and graded by professionals including Directors, Actors and Writers from the Film industry.
Mehfilm
(Pre-event)
A movie night under the stars. Gentle breezes, good conversation and a magical night under the blanket of skies.
CSR Initiative
(World Disability Day)
Team Frames visitied ETC Centre of Disability and performed various fun activites with the children there.
